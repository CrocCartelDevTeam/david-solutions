How often should I clean my dryer vent?
At least once a year. Larger families, frequent laundry, and pets may need cleaning every 6-9 months to prevent dangerous lint buildup.
How often should my chimney be swept?
At least once a year, ideally before fireplace season, to remove flammable creosote and check for damage.
How often should air ducts be cleaned?
Every 3-5 years for most homes, or sooner with pets, allergies, recent renovations, or signs of mold and odors.
How often should gutters be cleaned?
At least twice a year for most Florida homes, and always before storm season. Homes with many trees may need more frequent cleaning.
